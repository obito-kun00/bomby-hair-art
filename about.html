About Bombay Hair Art

Home About Us
Est. 2014

A Decade of Artistry

Founded in 2014 by master stylist Arjun Mehta, Bombay Hair Art was born from a singular vision — to bring world-class luxury salon experiences to the heart of Rajkot. What began as a modest studio on Yagnik Road has grown into Gujarat's most celebrated premium salon, serving thousands of discerning clients who demand nothing but the best.

Over the past decade, we have assembled a team of 15+ internationally trained stylists, colorists, and skin specialists who share our obsession with perfection. We invest in continuous education, premium products, and state-of-the-art equipment to ensure every client walks out feeling transformed, confident, and extraordinary. Our salon is more than a place — it's an experience.

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To deliver an unparalleled luxury salon experience that combines artistic excellence, premium products, and genuine care — making every client feel seen, valued, and beautifully transformed. We are committed to raising the standard of professional beauty services in Gujarat.

Our Vision

To become India's most trusted luxury salon brand — known not just for exceptional results, but for the warmth, expertise, and artistry that defines every interaction. We envision a future where Bombay Hair Art is synonymous with beauty, confidence, and transformation across the nation.

Our Journey

A Decade of Milestones

2014
The Beginning

Arjun Mehta founded Bombay Hair Art in a small studio on Kasturba Road, Rajkot, with a team of 3 passionate stylists and a dream to redefine luxury grooming in Gujarat.

2016
First Industry Award

Won the prestigious "Best New Salon — Gujarat" award at the India Salon & Wellness Awards, putting Bombay Hair Art on the national map.

2018
Expansion & Growth

Expanded to a larger, fully redesigned luxury space with 12 styling stations, a dedicated color bar, and a premium skin care suite. Team grew to 10 specialists.

2020
Digital Transformation

Launched online booking system and digital consultation services, making luxury salon access easier than ever for our growing clientele across Rajkot.

2022
100th Award Milestone

Celebrated winning our 100th industry award — a testament to our unwavering commitment to excellence, innovation, and client satisfaction over 8 years.

2024
New Flagship Location

Opened our stunning new flagship salon — a 3,000 sq ft luxury space featuring private styling suites, a bridal lounge, and the most advanced hair technology in Gujarat.
